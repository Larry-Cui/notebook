After tons of trial and error, I came across this python based theme - [Material for Mkdocs](https://squidfunk.github.io/mkdocs-material){:target="\_blank"} and decided for now to use it as my personal static website template.

I'm writing all articles/posts from scratch, hoping to keep a fraction of memory in writing, which are of my ideas, my feeling, and my reflection of life as I make my along.

I'm not expecting you can find this site, nor do I look forward to you enjoying it. But if you find some part might be helpful or inspiring, that alone is enough for me as the greatest reward.

In case I don't see ya again, good afternoon, good evening, and good night!
