    Special Formality of Remote Signing

    Canadian Legal Information Institute, or Canlii, hosts registration documents for land transfer for all provinces and territories, including Alberta.

    This post extracts the most common transfer documents for remote signing from its sub-page. If you want to see a full list of documents, please click here.

    For Sellers

    • Transfer1
    • Affidavit of Attestation for in-person signing
    • Affidavit of Attestation for remote signing
    • Affidavit Verifying Corporate Signing Authority
    • Affidavit remotely Verifying Corporate Signing Authority

    For Buyers

    • Affidavit re Value
    • Affidavit re Value for remote signing

    1. The transfer document is same for both in-person or remote signing ↩
